3D Printed Personal Protective Equipment.

  • March 30, 2020
  • By Abhishek Babu

This project involved 3D printing accpeted designs of masks and face shield frames and donating them to hospitals in the local area.

I used tools such as Blender and Ultimaker Cura to refine and slice the models. I then printed the models using my Creality CR-10 3D printer. I worked with a team at Microsoft that tracked need to donate the masks to those who needed them.
