About Me.

I am an undergraduate Computer Science student at the Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Washington. Through my undergraduate education at the Allen school, I have been able to explore and build my interests in data science and machine learning. These fields offer an avenue for a quantifiable and data-driven decision-making process in the fields they are applied in. I look forward to developing my skills in these areas, which will drive more insightful observations and deliver actionable intelligence in various industries.

From a young age, I have had a strong aptitude in mathematics. As I progressed through high school, I also took a keen interest in physics and computer science, and excelled in all three subjects. Soon after graduation, I am planning to pursue a Master's in computer science and to take advanced coursework that would help me pursue a career in research and development. In the future, I am also considering pursuing a Ph.D. in interdisciplinary research in computer science and aerospace, as I also have a keen interest in the aerospace field.
