More About Me.

How I Got Here.

I was first introduced to programming in the 9th grade. My first project was a Java Swing calculator. Throughout my high school career, I excelled at physics and mathematics, and wanted to leverage those subjects by pursuing aerospace engineering in college.

However, when I started at UW, as I took my aerospace prerequisite classes, I also took a few computer science courses and found them really interesting and rewarding. So, when I applied to the aerospace engineering program, I also applied to the computer science department on the off-chance that I would get in. When I got in to the computer science program, I still didn't have a result from the aerospace department. I decided to join the computer science program because I was aware of how well-respected and robust UW's computer science program is, along with my newfound interest in computer science.
