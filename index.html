Profile.

I'm a highly enthusiastic and inquisitive computer science student with a strong foundation in mathematics and programming logic. I am very interested in leveraging my skills in the fields of data science and machine learning.

As I was graduating high school, I wanted to pursue aerospace engineering and even got accepted to the University of Washington's Direct to College program for the College of Engineering. In my first year, as I was completing my aerospace prequisite classes, I also decided to take the first few introductory computer science courses because I was already familiar with Java and because this is always good knowledge to have in a STEM field. As I applied to the Aerospace Engineering major, I also decided to apply to the Computer Science program because I thought that the introductory courses were very interesting and I had performed very well in them. I wasn't expecting to be accepted, especially given how competitive the Computer Science major at UW is.

Well, I got accepted and decided to pursue Computer Science. I dove headfirst into the material and the great teaching staff at the Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science and Engineering have made this experience very rewarding. I am excited to continue my journey on this path and I am hoping to pursue the combined BS/MS program for Computer Science at UW.

I am also specializing in Data Science for my degree. Additionally, I'm also pursuing a minor in math.
