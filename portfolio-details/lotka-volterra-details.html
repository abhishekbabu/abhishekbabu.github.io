Analysis of Lotka-Volterra Model

Project information

  • Category: Other
  • Date(s): April 2017 - October 2017
  • Document: OneDrive

This is was my International Baccalaureate (IB) Extended Essay in Higher Level Mathematics. I consider the dynamics implied by the Lotka-Volterra predator-prey population model and propose modifications to improve the model's adherence to observed real-world preadtor-prey population dynamics. I received an A for this Extended Essay, the highest possible grade.
