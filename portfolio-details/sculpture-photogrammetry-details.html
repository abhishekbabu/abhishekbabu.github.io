Sculpture Photogrammetry

Project information

  • Category: Hardware
  • Date(s): March 2019

I used 3DF Zephyr, COLMAP, Meshlab and Meshmixer to photogrammetrically create a 3D model of a sculpture. I used Blender to create a base for the sculpture and Ultimaker Cura to slice the model. I printed the custom model with my Creality CR-10 3D printer.
