Foldable Solar Charger

I remixed and 3D printed a 3D model of a foldable triple solar panel holder using TinkerCAD and Ultimaker Cura to act as a USB charger. I soldered the three panels together with the a step-up USB converter to allow the user to connect a USB cable and charge any device of their liking.

Project information

  • Category: Hardware
  • Date(s): June 2020
Components
  • Original Foldable Solar Panel Holder STL files
  • 30AWG Wires
    • Used about 3.5 ft of each of the red and black wires.
  • 130x150mm 2.5W 5V/500mAh Solar Panel x3
    • These solar panels were mounted and soldered together to act as power source.
  • Black Carbon Fiber Vinyl Sheet Roll x1
    • Pieces of the roll were used to cover the exposed backside of the solar panels.
  • USB DC-DC 3V to 5V 2A Step Up Converter x1
    • Used to convert the power from the solar panels to USB compatible 5V output.
  • Solder | Soldering Iron | Iron Holder
    • Used to solder the wires to the panels and the USB converter.
  • Tape
    • Used to secure the wires on the back of the panels.
  • Hot Glue Sticks + Hot Glue Gun
    • Used to secure the USB converter in its slot in the charger.
