PlaDIS (Plant Disease Identification System)

Project information

  • Category: Software
  • Hackathon: Hack'20
  • Date(s): August 2020
  • Source code: github.com/kzhu7/PlaDIS
  • Partner(s): Kevin Zhu

The project is an Android application that detects diseases in plants from pictures of their leaves. We leveraged Google Cloud Platform and AutoML Vision Edge from Google Firebase to train a vision model that could detect these diseases. We curated part of this dataset from Kaggle for the training, testing and validation of the model. We built an accompanying Android application that allows users to take pictures of their plant leaves and then remotely accesses the Firebase-hosted model to produce the results of the diagnosis.
